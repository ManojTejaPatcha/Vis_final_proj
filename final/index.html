What you'll see in your results

Three things the agent doesn't want you to know.

01

Are you overpaying — and by how much?

A clear red, amber, or green verdict at the top of your results tells you instantly. It shows the exact dollar amount you could save per year by switching to a better plan for your specific drug.

02

What the agent isn't telling you

Some plans require prior authorization or step therapy for your medication — meaning your access could be delayed by weeks or you may be forced to try a different drug first. These requirements rarely come up during the sales call.

03

Exactly what to say back

For each manipulation tactic detected, you get a ready-to-use script: the specific question to ask the agent to shift the conversation back to objective data. You don't need to memorize anything.

Your privacy

Everything stays on your device. Always.

🔒

No account needed

You never log in, create a profile, or hand over personal data. Everything you enter stays in your browser and is never sent anywhere.

📡

No backend, no server

PlanPilot is 100% client-side. The analysis runs entirely in your browser tab — there is no server receiving your age, medications, or insurance details.

🗑

Delete anytime

Any information saved for learning (your past outcomes) lives in your browser's localStorage. Clear your browser data at any time to wipe it completely.
